Global Village

The Global Village is a sensational shopping spectacle and a seasonal attraction that happens every year, during the winter months of the city of Dubai. As a shopping extravaganza, this annual event features more than twenty five international pavilions, each showcasing a particular country’s products and culture.

Known as the UAE’s biggest shopping marketplace, this annual event is also considered a funfair with a good selection of amusement rides suitable for both kids and adults, including Ferris wheels, coasters, bumper cars, and many more. Good luck if you can tour this Dubai attraction in one day, cause I doubt if you can given the huge area.
